Quadratics

In order to solve a quadratic you need to get everything on one side so that the sum of everything equals zero
Ex: 2x2+4x+10 = 2x+20
2x2+4x-(2x)+ 10 = 2x-(2x)+20
Subtract 2x from both sides
2x2+2x+10-(20) = 20 - (20)
Subtract 20 from both sides
2x2+2x-10 = 0
